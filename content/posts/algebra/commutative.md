Let \(A\) be a [set](/zettelkasten/posts/set_theory/set). We call a [binary
operation](/zettelkasten/posts/algebra/binary_operation)
\(\ast: A \times A \rightarrow A\) _commutative_ if for every \(a,b \in A\):
\[
    a \ast b=b \ast a
\]

title = 'Ordered Pair'
+++

Let \(a\) and \(b\) be arbitrary objects. We define the _ordered pair_
\((a,b)\) of \(a\) and \(b\) to be the
[set](/zettelkasten/definitions/set_theory/set) defined by:
\[
    (a,b)=\{\{a\}, \{a,b\}\}
\]

We remark that the term _object_ is taken to be undefined.

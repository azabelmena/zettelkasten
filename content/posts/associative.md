Let \(A\) be a {{< backlink "set" "set" >}}. We call a {{< backlink "binary_operation"
"binary operation" >}}
\(\ast: A \times A \rightarrow A\) _associative_ if for every \(a,b,c \in A\):
\[
    (a \ast b) \ast c = a \ast (b \ast c)
\]

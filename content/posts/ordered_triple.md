Let \(a\) and \(b\), \(c\) be arbitrary objects. We define the
_ordered triple_ \((a,b,c)\) to be the
{{< backlink "ordered_pair" "ordered pair" >}}  \(((a,b),c)\).

{{< sidenote "remark" >}}
We remark that the term _object_ is taken to be undefined.
{{< /sidenote >}}
